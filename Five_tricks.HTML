Trick 1 – MA Trend Direction Trick

Use Moving Averages to choose direction only: BUY or SELL.

Settings
  • Fast MA (Yellow): e.g. 9 or 10 period.
  • Slow MA (Blue): e.g. 21 or 25 period.
  • Use on 1-min or 5-min chart with same logic.
Rules
  • BUY ONLY if price is above both MAs and fast MA is above slow MA.
  • SELL ONLY if price is below both MAs and fast MA is below slow MA.
  • NO TRADE if price is stuck between the two MAs.
Example
Example – Buy Trend:
Price is clearly above both yellow and blue MAs. Yellow MA is sloping up and above blue. You see a new green candle bouncing from yellow MA.
➜ You look only for BUY setups (use other tricks for entry).
Above both MAs → BUY zone
Below both MAs → SELL zone
Between MAs → No trade

Trick 2 – Big Wick Rejection Trick

Find long wicks at top or bottom to catch reversals.

What to Look For
  • SELL Setup: Candle with a very long top wick and small body near resistance.
  • BUY Setup: Candle with a very long bottom wick and small body near support.
Entry Rules
  1. Wait for the long wick candle to form at key level.
  2. Next candle must show direction:
    • After long top wick → next candle red → SELL.
    • After long bottom wick → next candle green → BUY.
Extra Confirmation
  • RSI above 70 → prefer SELL on long top wick.
  • RSI below 30 → prefer BUY on long bottom wick.
  • Always check MA: trade with overall trend when possible.
Example – Sell Wick:
Market is in uptrend, price hits resistance, you see a green candle with a huge top wick and small body. Next candle opens and turns red.
➜ Enter SELL on the red candle – stop above the wick.

Trick 3 – RSI 90% Entry Trick

RSI cross back from overbought/oversold + candle colour.

Settings
  • RSI Period: 14
  • Upper Level: 70
  • Lower Level: 30
BUY Rules
  1. RSI moves below 30 (oversold).
  2. Then RSI comes back above 30.
  3. The candle on chart is green → BUY.
SELL Rules
  1. RSI moves above 70 (overbought).
  2. Then RSI comes back below 70.
  3. The candle on chart is red → SELL.
Extra Safety
  • Prefer BUY when price is near support or above slow MA.
  • Prefer SELL when price is near resistance or below slow MA.
  • Avoid trades when RSI is between 40–60 (sideways zone).
Example – RSI Buy:
RSI drops to 25, then climbs back to 32. At that moment, you see a strong green candle at support.
➜ Enter BUY. Stop below recent low.
